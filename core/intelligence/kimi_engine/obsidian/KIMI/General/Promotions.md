# 🎉 Kimi K2.6 Launch Top-Up Rebate 🎉

## Event Time

**2026-04-19 08:00:00 (PST) – 2026-05-03 08:59:59 (PST)**

## Event Details

Make a single top-up of ≥ \$100 to receive tiered voucher bonuses based on the top-up amount.

| Top-up Amount | Voucher Bonus |
| ------------- | ------------- |
| \< \$100      | 0%            |
| $100 – $299   | 20%           |
| $300 – $999   | 25%           |
| ≥ \$1,000     | 30%           |

## Rules

* **Participation Limit**: During the campaign period, each Organization ID is eligible to participate only once and will receive a single voucher reward. The system will automatically identify the single transaction with the highest value among all top-ups made by the user during the campaign period (specifically, the highest-value transaction occurring on the day of the user's first top-up within the period); the reward will be calculated proportionally based on this specific transaction, while all other top-ups will not qualify for rewards.

* **Eligibility**: Eligibility is determined based on the actual timestamp at which the Open Platform receives the top-up funds. Eligibility for this specific campaign is calculated independently and is unrelated to any previous top-up reward promotions.
